About Me

Hi, my name is Michaela! I’m 16 years old, a competitive figure skater, and a junior in high school. I spend most of my time at the rink training and working toward something that means everything to me.

When I’m not training, I’m usually doing schoolwork or in the kitchen. Baking has become one of my biggest hobbies — not just because I love sweets, but because I love the process of creating something from scratch.

I’ve always seen food as both fuel and enjoyment. As an athlete, I’m constantly thinking about how what I eat affects how I train, recover, and perform — but I also believe you shouldn’t have to give up the things you enjoy.

That’s why I started Fuel & Flour Co. — to create desserts that feel balanced, intentional, and worth it. A way to enjoy something sweet without feeling like you're working against your goals.

This is more than just baking for me. Every order directly supports my training and helps me keep going.

So truly, every single order means more than you know 🤍
