About Me

Hi, my name is Michaela! I’m 16 years old, a competitive figure skater, and a junior in high school. I spend most of my time at the rink training and working toward something that means everything to me. When I’m not training, I’m usually doing schoolwork or snacking :)

If you ask any of my friends or family, they’ll tell you I love food. To me, it’s both fuel for training and something I genuinely enjoy. I’ve always had a bit of a sweet tooth, and baking has become one of my favorite things.

I started Fuel & Flour Co. as a way to bring those parts of my life together — sharing something I love while supporting something that means so much to me. Everything I make is homemade, small-batch, and made with care.

Every order helps support my training and keeps me moving toward my dream. Even small orders truly make a difference, and I’m so grateful for every bit of support 🤍
